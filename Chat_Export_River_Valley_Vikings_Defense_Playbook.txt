Aggressive with discipline.
We attack protections, formations, and tendencies to create negative plays. Aggression is a tool, not a habit. When the moment calls for it, we call off the horses and force the offense to be patient.

Box the run.
We set hard edges, compress inside gaps, and keep the ball in our help. Everyone fits with clarity. If the front stunts or pressures, fits change—but they never become confused.

Relentless pursuit and tackling.
Effort is non-negotiable. We rally with correct angles and finish tackles with leverage, wrap, and drive. We eliminate yards after contact.

Leverage wins.
We protect outside leverage against perimeter threats and QB run. We protect inside leverage against quick game, crossers, and RPO access routes. Safeties are fixers—get everyone aligned, eliminate busts, and finish explosives.

No uncovered gaps / stunts and pressures still fit sound.

No free releases when called / deny access and communicate bunch/stack.

No missed tackles / leverage + wrap + finish.

No penalties that extend drives.

No loafs / pursuit is mandatory on film.
